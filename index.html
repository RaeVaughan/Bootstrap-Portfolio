About Me

Hi! My name is Rachel, and I'm an aspiring web developer and lover of lists, books, cats, yoga, lifting, beer, and chocolate. In my spare time, you can find me at the gym, out on a hike on one of Austin's many fabulous trails, reading, binging Netflix, or cuddling with my cat, Kiki. I also tend to stand on my hands (in any way that's not a handstand - still working on that one!) when I think no one is looking!
